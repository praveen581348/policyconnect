Frequently Asked Questions

What is the claim process?

In case of an emergency or claim requirement, simply call our 24/7 helpline or the insurance provider's toll-free number. We assist you in gathering documents and coordinating with the hospital or service provider to ensure a smooth cashless or reimbursement claim.

Are Mutual Funds better than Fixed Deposits?

While FDs offer guaranteed returns, Mutual Funds have the potential to provide higher returns over the long term by investing in market-linked instruments (Equity or Debt). The choice depends on your risk appetite and investment horizon.

How secure is my personal information?

At PolicyConnect, we use industry-standard encryption protocols. Your personal and financial details are strictly confidential and shared only with verified insurance/investment providers for processing your requests.

How long does it take for a callback?

Once you submit an enquiry on our portal, one of our expert financial advisors will typically call you back within 2 to 4 working hours.

Can I update my request after submission?

Yes, once our advisor calls you, you can clarify any changes or add more details to your enquiry. We also provide an update feature in the admin dashboard for our processing team.
